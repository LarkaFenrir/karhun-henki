## Philosophy
The brand **Karhun Henki** (Finnish: __"Spirit of the Bear"__) originates from my connection with nature, its inhabitants, and my mission to protect.

The Bear has always been an animal spiritually close to me, and with which I share key aspects of its personality: **resilience, introspection, strength, protection**.

Under the **Karhun Henki** brand, I aim to build apps to **protect** the defenseless, and to **empower** them.
